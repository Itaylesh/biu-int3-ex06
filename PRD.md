## Product / Idea Description

**Kavana** (כוונה — "intention" / "focus") is a fictional AI deep-work companion that protects uninterrupted focus time and auto-orchestrates a knowledge worker's day. The product is presented through a ~60-second programmatic marketing/explainer video built with Remotion.

Kavana is not a real product — it is a polished fictional concept designed to demonstrate a complete Vibe Coding multimedia production pipeline for the university assignment.

---

## Problem Statement

Knowledge workers lose hours daily to fragmented attention: cascading notifications, context-switching between apps, and manually juggling calendars and task lists. The emotional cost is invisible until productivity metrics turn red — but the damage is cumulative.

**Visual manifestation (Show, Don't Tell):** A dashboard fills with pulsing notification cards; a focus meter drains from green to red; calendar blocks fracture into tiny fragments.

---

## Proposed Solution

Kavana acts as an AI shield and orchestrator:

1. **Focus Shield** — Silences non-critical interruptions during deep-work blocks.
2. **Smart Orchestration** — Reschedules meetings and batches communications automatically.
3. **Clarity Dashboard** — Surfaces one clear priority at a time with calm, minimal UI.

The video communicates this through six structured scenes driven by `data/scenes.json`.

## Functional Requirements

- Remotion composition reads validated JSON scene data at build time.
- Each scene renders distinct animated visuals (no external image APIs).
- Transitions: slide-in, fade, scale between scenes.
- Text overlays readable at 1920×1080.
- Audio track synced to composition duration.
- zod schema validates all JSON fields before rendering.

---

## Visual Requirements

- **Resolution:** 1920×1080, 30fps
- **Palette:** Deep navy `#0B1220` → violet `#6366F1` → accent cyan `#22D3EE`
- **Typography:** Inter (English), Heebo (Hebrew) via `@remotion/google-fonts`
- **Style:** Clean motion design — gradients, geometric shapes, animated charts, device mockups — all generated in React/CSS
- **Animations:** Staggered entrance, counter animations, progress bars, notification cascade
